Time Slot Converter

Welcome to the Time Slot Converter! Setting up meetings can be a pain when your colleagues or clients live in different timezones, and we're here to help.

Enter the date, time, and duration of each time slot, and your openings will be added to a time slot table in a readable format.

Once you've added your first meeting, add a timezone with our timezone picker and your time slots will be displayed in additional timezones.

Remove a time slot or timezone at any time!

1. Enter the date, time, and duration of a time slot

2. Click "Add time slot"

3. Add additional time slots or timezones to the table

4. Remove time slots or timezones as needed!

Removed time slots must be added back through the form!

Clearing the time slot table cannot be undone!
